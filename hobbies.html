Hobbies

What is a hobby ?

A hobby is a regular activity that is done for enjoyment. A list of hobbies is lengthy and always changing as interests and fashions change. Hobbies can bring in enthusiasm and a pleasure that enriches life. If you haven’t found the right hobby, then keep looking. The “pursuit of happiness” is half the fun. There are several different types of hobbies to help you narrow down what you’re interested in doing with your spare time. Some of these hobbies are.

You can start by choosing an hobbie:

  • Enrichment Hobbies The hobby that helps you enrich/improve your mind. Hobbies include, Foreign Language Study, Reading, Blogging, Writing Etc. Enrichment Hobbies.
  • Sports and Games As everyone know, a physical activity to burn stress and build health. Like jogging, Horseback Riding, Yoga and other team sports. Sport and Games.
  • Social Activities A hobby that sometimes benefits most from a hobby that involves friends and/or meeting new people. Like Card Games, Dinner/Movie Club etc Social Activities.
  • Creative Hobbies A Creative hobby is a good way to find out what we are made up of. A Creative hobby benefits a many of them. Hobbies Include Drawing/painting/photography, Scrapbooking. Creative Hobbies.
  • Collecting Collecting items that you find intresting and adding them to your collection is a fun, goal oriented pursuit. Like: Antiques, Decor, Postcards. Collecting.
  • Making and tinkering Making a replica of a real object in a smaller scale goes back to prehistoric times with small clay “dolls” and Tinkering is ‘dabbling’ with the making process, often applied to the major hobby of tinkering with car repairs, and various kinds of restoration. Hobbies Like Modelling, 3D Printing, Dress Making. Making and tinkering.
  • Outdoor recreation Outdoor pursuits are the group of activities which occur outdoors. These hobbies include gardening, hill walking, hiking, backpacking, cycling. Outdoor recreation.
  • Domestic hobbies The activity that you perform as a duty, doesn’t mean that you might not thoroughly enjoy opportunities to explore and create new ideas. Hobbies include Cooking, Knitting etc. Domestic hobbies.
